A little tour
Hi! Welcome to our web page. We are an independent zero-budget(for now) studio made of people and close friends all around the world united by one purpose. We are passionate gamers and furious enthusiasts with a dream to create our masterpiece and form a comerсial game studio to develop games for a living.

NO GUNS ALOWED

EASY TO LEARN, EASY TO MASTER

The only thing you'll need to play is one finger — just swipe accross the screen and you'll get where you need, it's that easy! But take more time to learn it, and you'll turn this simple move in an elegant deadly weapon, slicing the enemies on clips worth sharing with friends!

TRAVERSE THE GALAXY

You get the opportunity to play as an operator of cutting-edge combat android in a galaxy-renown private security company. Securing ships, executing sabotages and rescuing hostages will be usual tasks for you. And all while travelling through various spaceships — from small cargo shuttles to planet-sized casino cities!

FIND YOUR ONE WAY

You are fast, you are deadly, but you are also vulnerable. Use your wits and skills to overcome overwhelming obstacles in a smart way. Slam doors to surprise your enemy, hang on to walls to prepare an ambush, use terminals to open shortcuts — imagination is the only limit!

A short story
You are a combat android operator of a space-renown private security company. Eliminating space pirates, capturing ships and performing different sabotage operations are usual tasks for you. Roam around space, visit different places: from cargo ships to huge space casino cities...
Visual style
Our original intention was to create a game with 16-bit pixel art. However, we saw that the indie market was oversaturated with games in such style, so we decided to seek inspiration elsewhere. Stumbling upon such phenomena as PS1 demakes, we eventually realized the project as a 2.5D platformer, in a visual manner similar to retro games from the early 3D era.
publishing problems
Developing games requires some time and resources. We as human beings have to live somewhere, eat and pay bills for electricity and the internet (we don't need anything more to make the game lmao). So we are looking for some small investment so we can focus on our game instead of having full-time jobs :D
