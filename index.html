Hi! Welcome to our web page. We are an independent zero-budget studio made of people all around the world. We are passionate gamers and furious enthusiasts. Here we are creating alongside with the community. We are trying to to make the development process as clear as we can for our audience and allow outside people to contribute to the project. So feel free to get in touch with us and join our social networks!

// You have a limited amount of dash charges that recharge over time, so make sure you do not get caught in a bad position without any.

// If an enemy walks into you, or shoots you, it is game over. You can dash into enemies and even into their bullets, satisfyingly slicing your enemies and reflecting their bullets back at them.
